Who We Are

Alpha's Pizza is an independent fasts food joint that specializes in pizza making. Established in 2022, Alpha's Pizza is growing to be the preferred pizza place in Accra, Ghana.We offer delivery, pickup and dine-in services. At Alpha's Pizza, we believe that everybody deserves good pizzalicious pizzas that are prepared under good hygienic conditions.We give you the chance to have your pizza the way you'd like it by allowing you the freedom to customise your toppings.
